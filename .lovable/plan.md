
# Boost Profits LLC — Cash Flow SaaS Marketing Website

## Design Direction
- **Palette**: Navy (#0D1B2A primary), Steel Blue (#1B4965), Coral (#FF6B6B accents/CTAs), Light Gray (#F0F4F8 backgrounds), White
- **Typography**: Inter for body, bold geometric headings, clean spacing inspired by LangChain
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover scale on buttons, card lift + shadow on hover, subtle parallax on hero

## Pages & Sections (Single-page with anchor navigation)

### 1. Navigation
- Sticky header: Logo + links (Features, How It Works, Pricing, FAQ) + "Start Free Trial" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Headline: "Stop Losing Money to Late Payments"
- Subheadline: "Boost Profits LLC helps businesses collect unpaid invoices faster — guaranteed."
- Two CTAs: "Start Free Trial" (coral) + "Learn How It Works" (outline)
- Dashboard mockup placeholder with subtle gradient background and parallax effect

### 3. Trusted By / Social Proof
- Row of generic industry logos (Retail, Services, E-commerce, Healthcare, Consulting)
- 2-3 italic testimonial quotes beneath

### 4. Features Grid (6 cards)
- Automated Reminders, Prioritized Follow-up, Cash Flow Dashboard, Partial Payments, Results Guarantee, Easy Integration
- Icons + title + description, hover lift animation

### 5. How It Works (3 steps)
- Connect Your Invoices → Automate & Track → Get Paid Faster
- Numbered steps with icons and connecting line/arrows

### 6. Dashboard Preview
- Laptop/tablet mockup with placeholder dashboard screenshot
- Caption: "Insightful cash flow analytics and invoice tracker"

### 7. Pricing Section
- Monthly/Annual toggle
- Starter ($49/mo or $490/yr) and Premium ($99/mo or $990/yr) cards
- Feature lists, "Start Free Trial" buttons, 30-day money-back guarantee note

### 8. Testimonials (5 quotes)
- Carousel or grid of founder-style quotes from diverse industries

### 9. FAQ Section
- Collapsible accordion: ~5 common questions with answers

### 10. Contact Section
- Simple form: Name, Email, Message
- Company contact info

### 11. Footer
- Links: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, social icons
- Cookie consent banner with Accept button + Privacy Policy link
- GDPR-compliant email opt-in note

## Technical Details
- SEO meta tags (title, description, keywords)
- Semantic HTML with ARIA labels
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- All sections as separate React components for maintainability
